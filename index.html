Resume Game - Submission for Mintbean.io hackathon.


Mintbean challenged participants to create an action game in one week using Phaser. The theme was action. My personal take on an action...is keeping up the grind and sending out resumes! What's more actionable than that!??



The context: You are a software developer, trying to get a job! You musn't be discouraged by rejections. You must keep shooting out resumes!

This game is simple and rewarding. You just keep clicking at the icons until someone accepts your resume. The first 3 mintbeans that you hit will double the population of the other logos. The game is simple but the main takeaway is that perseverance is what counts. Don't give up!
