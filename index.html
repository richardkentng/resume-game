Resume Game

Submission for Mintbean.io hackathon.


Mintbean challenged participants to create an action game in one week using Phaser. The theme was action. My personal take on an action...is keeping up the grind and sending out resumes! What's more actionable than that!??



The context~  You are a software developer, trying to get a job! You musn't be discouraged by rejections. You must keep pushing out resumes!

This game itself is quite simple. You just keep clicking at the icons until your resume catches someone's eye! The first 3 mintbeans that you hit will double the population of the other logos. They're hard to miss! Anyhow, the main takeaway of this game is that perseverance is what counts. Got to stick with it!
